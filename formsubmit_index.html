What is Koreapedia?

Koreapedia makes exploring Korea, learning the Korean language, making K-friends, and getting insights into all things Korean fun and engaging.

Who is Koreapedia for?

Koreaphiles, Overseas Koreans (you are us!), Korean Wannabies (Korea wants you!), Korean language learners, travelers, foreign residents, business people, and K-wavers.

How does the Koreapedia wiki work?

Our community-driven platform allows enthusiasts from Korea and around the world to contribute their Korean knowledge and experiences, creating a rich, diverse English language resource for all things Korean.

먹자 (mok-ja) Let's Eat!

Tasty way to learn about the vibrant world of Korean cuisine that is captivating foodies worldwide while practicing "food talk". Find kimchi lovers in your local area and form Kimchi Clubs.

보자 (bo-ja) Let's Watch!

Join online and in-person clubs for K-movie and K-drama screenings and discussions. Movies and dramas with dual-language subtitles are a seriously fun way to learn Korean and learn about Korea.

하자 (ha-ja) Let's Do!

Create or join online or in-person Let's Do! Teams to go places, learn things, and make friends. A shared passion with friends is a great way to practice Korean or English - whichever way you swing.
